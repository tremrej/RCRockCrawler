RockCrawler

This is a remix version of an RC Rock Crawler that I found on Thingiverse. The original model got deleted since then. I modified the gearbox to make it slower, hence with more torque.

Video: 1. In action 2. Showing the shock 3. Gearbox assembly

I found a link to the original video from the original designer: 1. Make an RC Rock Crawler - Part 1 2. Make an RC Rock Crawler - Part 2
